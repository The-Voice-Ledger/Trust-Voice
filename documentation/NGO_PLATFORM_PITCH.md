## Technology (Simple Explanation)

**Three Components:**

1. **Conversational AI** (ChatGPT-style + Local African Language Model)
   - **For European languages:** OpenAI GPT-4 (English, French, German, Italian)
   - **For African languages:** Addis AI (Amharic, Swahili) - trained on 2000+ hours of Ethiopian speech
   - Understands donor questions in any language
   - Provides instant campaign information
   - Processes donation requests

2. **Multi-Channel Interface**
   - WhatsApp, Telegram, phone calls
   - No app needed
   - Works on any device
   - Language set once during registration (not auto-detected)

3. **Blockchain Receipts** (Digital Proof)
   - Every donation gets unique digital receipt
   - Shows exactly where money went
   - Can't be altered or faked
   - Optional crypto payment processing

**No complex setup. Works on existing phones and payment systems.**

**Proven Technology:** Built on Voice Ledger architecture, successfully tracking 1000+ coffee farmers in Ethiopia with 98% accuracy.
