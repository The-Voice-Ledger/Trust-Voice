## Technology (Simple Explanation)

**Three Core Components:**

1. **Voice AI** (Natural Conversation)
   - Understand questions in English, Swahili, French
   - Extract intent: "donate", "browse", "back project"
   - Works via WhatsApp, Telegram, phone calls
   - ~8 seconds response time (goal: <3 seconds)

2. **Payment Processing** (Multi-Method, Multi-Currency)
   - **Donations IN:** Stripe (cards + bank transfers globally), M-Pesa (Kenya/Tanzania/Uganda)
   - **Payouts OUT:** M-Pesa B2C (instant), Bank transfers (SEPA/SWIFT), Stripe payouts
   - **Bridge global finance â†’ local impact:** Berlin donor's â‚¬ â†’ Tanzanian creator's KES (same day)
   - 31+ currencies supported (USD, EUR, GBP, KES, TZS, UGX, ZAR)
   - Lower fees: 1-3% vs traditional 7-10% (Western Union, banks)
   - **No middlemen, no correspondent banks, direct to communities**

3. **Blockchain Receipts** (Trust Layer)
   - Every donation/pledge = unique digital receipt
   - GPS-stamped photos from field
   - Stored on Ethereum (can't be faked)
   - Optional NFT certificates

**No complex setup. Works on existing phones and payment systems.**
